About Us

Our Story

Established in the 1980s, Shannon's is stylized after traditional Irish pubs. With soft lighting, beautifully carved wood seating, and a traditional wrap around bar seating area, guests will truly feel like they are visiting Ireland itself.

Our staff is committed to creating the full Irish experience and are ready to serve excellent, authentic Irish meals.

Catch us on special Irish holidays or for our weekly Thursday Trivia Night for unique Irish experiences including Irish traditional dancing, traditional instrumental performances, and even singing!



Our History

The Irish have long been an established group throughout the Chicagoland area. Shannon's delivers the authentic experience of our ancestors by serving traditional meals and creating a traditional atmosphere to immerse in.

First established by an Irish family who believed in keeping Irish traditions alive, the restaurant has flourished under the support of local customers and a heavy local Irish demographic ancestry.

Join us for some authentic meals and good alcohol served in the traditional Irish way.
