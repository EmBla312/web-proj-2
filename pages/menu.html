Our Menu

Our Food

Shannon's boasts an array of authentic Irish foods. Some of our favorites include shepherd's pie, Irish stew, fish and chips, and our classic onion rings.

We offer appetizers, soups, salads, burgers and sandwiches, steaks, and desserts. Every day our chef serves up a different soup so be sure to ask when dining.



Our Drinks

Nothing says Irish than a good ol' alcoholic beverage! At Shannon's we offer a variety of options including draughts, specialty draughts, and bottled beers. Of course, our Guinness is a staple and we encourage all customers to try it upon visiting.

For more drink options, ask a server about our featured bottled sections.
